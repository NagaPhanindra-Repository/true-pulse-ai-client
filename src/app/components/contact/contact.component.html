Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Create an account, create your first entity, describe your business or upload a PDF, and generate your first website. From there you can turn on hosting, chat, images, and events.

Is there a trial period?

Yes. New users can explore the core ezit.ai flow without a heavy setup process, so you can validate your entity experience before committing more deeply.

How is my data protected?

We use enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and regular security audits. Your data is your data.

Do you offer API access?

Yes. For advanced teams and enterprise workflows, we support deeper integration paths around memory, intelligence, and operational data.

Can I export my data?

Absolutely. You can export all your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV) at any time. No vendor lock-in.

What about teams and collaboration?

ezit.ai supports team workflows, operational updates, feedback loops, and shared context so more than one person can run the entity experience cleanly.
